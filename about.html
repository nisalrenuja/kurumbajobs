About Us


Kurumba Jobs is a Job directory where Job seekers can find and apply for Jobs all across Sri Lanka. Job seekers and submit your professional profile with the CV and we will match your with Jobs posted on Kurumba Jobs. Job seekers will receive instant Job Alerts to you Mobile Phone or Email ID soon when matching Jobs are published.


Employers can Post Job Listings for Free! Manage your Job listings through easy to use My account panel.


Your Job vacancies will receive added exposure through Kurumba’s Social media pages.
