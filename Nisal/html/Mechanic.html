Mechanic

Job Duties:
• Conduct regular maintenance on machinery and automotive systems
• Assemble mechanical components according to specifications
• Clean and apply lubricants to machinery components
• Troubleshoot reported problems and resolve them in a timely manner
• Examine machines and oversee diagnostic tests to determine functionality problems
• Design a plan of action for all maintenance tasks and upgrades
• Maintain work logs, repairs, and maintenance records
• Monitor inventory and order new parts when necessary
• Offer consultation on maintenance and preventative procedures to machine


Requirements:
• Male
• CGTTI / NVQ or Professional Qualification
• Age below 30
• 1 - 2 years experience
