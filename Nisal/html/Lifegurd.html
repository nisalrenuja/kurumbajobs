Lifeguard

Job Description:
• Smile and be friendly with colleagues, guests, and contractors.
• Handle complaints
• Consistently and uniformly enforce rules and restrictions
• Ride all rides, slides and attractions as required for operational purposes.
• Ensure guest, colleague and contractor safety at all times.
• Report any safety concerns, near misses, or incidents to management
• Work at heights, in water, and in extreme temperatures, outside, year-round.
• Follow all manufacturer and department guidelines and Standard Operating Procedures regarding attraction operation and guest safety.
• Maintain rescue skill competency and basic life support skill competency at “test ready” levels at all times.
